## General Structure
- username@domain.TLD
	- username = a string of characters to represent a login, usually your name or something related to you
	- domain = the website
	- TLD = .com, .net, .org, etc.
- Example:
	- 99haxemail@gmail.com
	- pxelblade@proton.me
	- pxelblade@duck.com
## History of Email
- The @ symbol in emails was adopted from an obsolete character on typewriters.
